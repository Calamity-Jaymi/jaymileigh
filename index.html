Design and Development

I discovered front end web design when I was about 13. Designing profile pages with HTML and CSS on MySpace and other forum and fan pages for bands I loved. I have always enjoyed designing web pages, but never knew that it was a career option for me until I was well into my 20's. Now I am self-teaching myself again to pivot my future to bring my fun and challenges into my life.

Cooking

Just being able to cook by feeling and heart, that gift runs in my family. For the last 12 years I have been in pursuit of the ultimate dream, running and owning my own food truck. But after running a restaurant, designing the menu, cooking, bartending, and just doing it all on my own I have come to realize that it takes the joy out of cooking. Better to learn early than before the investment, eh? At least I can still cook for the love of it.
